Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my package on fire?

add

We understand that this is an unusual and concerning situation. While our delivery process is designed to be safe and efficient, several factors can lead to unexpected incidents. Delivery vehicles, like any other machinery, can experience technical issues that might result in overheating or fires. Additionally, our delivery vehicles share the road with other drivers, and accidents involving other vehicles or objects can sometimes cause fires.

Do you offer next-day delivery?

add

Yes, we offer next-day delivery for most items! To qualify, please ensure you place your order before our cut-off time, typically 5 PM local time. Availability may vary based on your location and the specific items ordered. During checkout, you will see the next-day delivery option if it's available for your order. We strive to get your items to you as quickly and efficiently as possible. If you have any further questions about next-day delivery or need assistance with your order, please contact our customer service team.

What is your return policy?

add

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, we offer a hassle-free return policy. You can return most items within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange. Items must be returned in their original condition and packaging. To initiate a return, simply log into your account, navigate to the "Order History" section, and select the item you wish to return. Follow the prompts to print a return shipping label. If you need any assistance or have questions about the return process, please contact our customer service team. We're here to help!

How can I change or cancel my order?

add

We understand that sometimes plans change, and you may need to modify or cancel your order. If you need to make changes or cancel your order, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. If your order has not yet been processed for shipment, we will do our best to accommodate your request. Once an order has been shipped, changes or cancellations may not be possible, and you may need to follow our return policy after receiving the item. For the quickest assistance, please have your order number ready when you contact us.
